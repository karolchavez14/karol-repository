Follow this easy recipe and enjoy delicious homemade Coffee Cupcakes!

Ingredients (Cupcakes):

  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100 g) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup (110 g) brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (190 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) milk
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee (dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Ingredients (frosting):

  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (190 g) powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee (dissolved in 1 teaspoon hot water)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Alternately add dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, starting and ending with the flour mixture.
  6. Divide batter evenly among cupcake liners (about 12).
  7. Bake for 18‚Äì22 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool completely.
  8. For the frosting, beat the butter until creamy. Add powdered sugar, coffee, vanilla, and milk as needed. Beat until smooth.
  9. Frost the cooled cupcakes and enjoy!
