🍪 Sugar Cookies Recipe

Follow this easy recipe and enjoy delicious homemade Sugar cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200 g) white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 and 3/4 cups (340 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: extra sugar for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture until a dough forms.
  6. Roll dough into balls or flatten slightly for round cookies.
  7. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Optional: Sprinkle with sugar before baking.
  9. Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until edges are lightly golden.
  10. Let cool before decorating or serving.

These sugar cookies are perfect for holidays, parties, or a sweet everyday treat! ❄️🎉
