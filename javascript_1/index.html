What is chemistry?

Chemistry is the branch of science that studies the properties of matter and how it interacts with energy. It is considered a physical science and is related to physics. It is sometimes called the "core science" because it is an important part of other sciences, such as biology, earth science, and physics. Scientists who specialize in chemistry are called chemists.
