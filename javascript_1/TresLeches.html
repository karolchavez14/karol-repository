Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Follow this easy recipe and enjoy delicious homemade Chocolate Chip cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Add the butter, egg, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Form small dough balls and place them on a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
