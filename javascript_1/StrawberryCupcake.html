Follow this easy recipe and enjoy delicious homemade Coffee Cupcakes!

Ingredients (Cupcakes):

  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200 g) white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (190 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Ingredients (Filling & Topping):

  • 1 cup chopped fresh strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (for macerating strawberries)
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: extra sliced strawberries for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet mixture, alternating with milk, and mix until combined.
  6. Fill cupcake liners about 2/3 full and bake for 18‚Äì20 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cupcakes cool completely.
  8. Meanwhile, toss chopped strawberries with 1 tbsp sugar and let sit for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  9. Whip cream with powdered sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  10. Cut a small hole in the center of each cupcake and fill with strawberries.
  11. Top with whipped cream and a fresh strawberry slice. Serve chilled!
