Follow this easy recipe and enjoy delicious homemade Chocolate Chip cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100 g) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup (110 g) brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups (155 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 12 crushed Oreo cookies
  • 1/2 cup (85 g) white chocolate chips (optional)

  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C).
  2. Cream the butter and both sugars together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  6. Gently fold in the crushed Oreos and white chocolate chips (if using).
  7. Form dough balls and place on a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10‚Äì12 minutes, or until edges are lightly golden.
  9. Let cool on the tray for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
