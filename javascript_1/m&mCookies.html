Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200 g) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100 g) white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (310 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (250 g) M&M's (plus extra for topping)
  • Optional: 1/2 cup (90 g) chocolate chips

  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  6. Fold in M&M's (and chocolate chips if using).
  7. Form dough balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Press a few extra M&M's on top of each cookie for a colorful look.
