Follow this easy recipe and enjoy delicious homemade Raspberry Chocolate Cupcakes!

Ingredients (Cupcakes):

  • 3/4 cup (95 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (45 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup (135 g) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Ingredients (Raspberry Filling):

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water

Ingredients (Chocolate Frosting):

  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (45 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups (240 g) powdered sugar
  • 2‚Äì3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C). Line a muffin pan with cupcake liners.
  2. In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk sugar, oil, eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into wet until fully combined.
  5. Divide batter evenly into liners and bake for 16‚Äì18 minutes. Let cool completely.
  6. For the filling: cook raspberries, sugar, and cornstarch mixed with water in a small pot until thickened. Cool and strain if desired.
  7. Cut a small center hole in each cupcake and fill with raspberry filling.
  8. For frosting: beat butter and cocoa, add powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla. Beat until creamy.
  9. Frost cupcakes and top with a fresh raspberry or chocolate shavings!
